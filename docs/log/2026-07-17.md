# 2026-07-17

- **Audit & Review**: Conducted a deep architectural review of the compiler pipeline, focusing on semantic invariants, functions, and standard library loading.
- **Findings Logged**:
  - `BUG-11` (CRITICAL): Identified a simulator vs. synthesis mismatch where the shift operator `<<` dynamically expands widths during simulation instead of preserving the LHS width (violating the checker semantics in `checker/widths/ops.rs`). Logged to `docs/audit/bugs.md`.
  - `BUG-12` (HIGH): Identified a scoping flaw in `emit_verilog/module.rs` where module constants are intentionally stripped from `function automatic` bodies, breaking standard Verilog parameterization patterns. Logged to `docs/audit/bugs.md`.
  - `BUG-13` (MEDIUM): Documented the 128-bit architectural ceiling in the simulator (`Val` underpinned by `u128`), which presents silent data corruption risks for wider buses. Logged to `docs/audit/bugs.md`.
  - `SEC-5` (MEDIUM): Logged a potential path traversal vulnerability in the `[lib] std` override mechanism in `config.rs`. Unsandboxed overrides pose a risk in shared/cloud execution environments like the WASM playground. Logged to `docs/audit/security.md`.
